Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Simple Korean Dish that is very simple and delicous to make:

Ingredients

  • 2 bowls of steamed rice
  • 1 cup of chopped Kimchi
  • 2-3 tablespoons of GochuJang
  • 3 tablespoons of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 cup of Chopped up spam

Steps

  • Heat a wide Pan or Skillet. Add Vegetable Oil
  • Add Onion and cook until almost cooked
  • Add Kimchi, Spam and cook for 2-3 mins
  • Finally add rice (best if leftover rice) and stir for 5 mins
  • Add an egg and seaweed on top (optional)
