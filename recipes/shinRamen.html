Shin Ramen Recipe

Description:

Simple Shin Ramen Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet Shin Ramen
  • 1-2 Eggs
  • 1 and Half cups of water
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Put 1 and half cups of water in a pot
  2. add the powder and the dried vegetable packets from the Shin Ramen Pack
  3. Boild until the water is bubbling HEAVILY
  4. Add Noodles and cover the pot
  5. After 3 minutes check the noodle consistency
  6. Crack the eggs in and stir until you can see the white bits of egg
  7. Cook for another 2 minutes
    8. OPTIONAL:
    • Eat with Kimchi for best taste
    • After eating the noodles you can add some rice and have Shin Porrige
