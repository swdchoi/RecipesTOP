Kimchi Stew Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Kimchi
  • 500g of pork belly
  • 3 Cups of Water
  • Spring Onions
  • 100g tofu
  • 2 spoons of Gochu Jang

Steps:

  1. Add water and GochuJang into a pot and boil
  2. cook the pork belly in a pan with cooking oil till half cooked
  3. add the Kimchi and 500 grams of pork belly to the pot and boil for 10 mins
  4. add the tofu and spring onions and cover for 4 mins
  5. Enjoy with rice!
